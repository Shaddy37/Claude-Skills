## Reel Types

### Type 1: Tutorial Reel

**Structure:**
```
0-3s: Hook (result you'll get)
3-15s: Step-by-step demo
15-30s: CTA + call to follow
```

**Hook Examples:**
- "How to automate [X] in 30 seconds"
- "This tool saved me 20 hours/week"
- "POV: You discovered n8n"

**Script Template:**
```
[HOOK - 3 sec]
"Here's how to [RESULT] in under a minute:"

[STEP 1 - 5 sec]
"First, [ACTION]..."

[STEP 2 - 5 sec]
"Then, [ACTION]..."

[STEP 3 - 5 sec]
"Finally, [ACTION]..."

[RESULT - 5 sec]
"And that's it! Now you [RESULT]."

[CTA - 5 sec]
"Follow for more automation tips 💡"
```

## Reels Best Practices

### Video Specs
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds optimal
- **Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Quality:** 1080p minimum
- **Audio:** Clear voice, music optional

### Cover Image
- Bold text (title)
- High contrast
- 1080x1920
- Stop-scroll energy

### Hook Formula
- Lead with RESULT
- Use "POV" or "How to"
- Promise specific value
- Stop curiosity

### Engagement
- Reply to comments
- Use trending audio (when relevant)
- Add to stories
- Engage with similar creators
