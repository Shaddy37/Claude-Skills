# 📈 Engagement Tips - 2026 Algorithm

## What the Algorithm Rewards (2026)

### Priority Signals

| Rank | Signal | Weight | Action |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Saves** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Create referenceable content |
| 2 | **Shares** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Make content worth sending |
| 3 | **Comments** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | End with questions |
| 4 | **Likes** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Secondary |
| 5 | **Follows** | ⭐⭐ | From DMs + bio |

---

## Why Saves Win

- **Save rate** is THE metric in 2026
- **Saves = "This is valuable"** signal
- **Algorithm prioritizes** saved content
- **Evergreen content** gets more saves

### How to Get More Saves
1. Make content **referenceable**
2. Include **"Save this"** CTAs
3. Create **useful guides**
4. Make **listicles** (people save lists)
5. Add **checklists**
6. Provide **templates**

---

## Content That Gets Saves

| Content Type | Save Rate |
|--------------|-----------|
| Complete guides | High |
| How-to tutorials | High |
| Listicles | High |
| Tool comparisons | Medium |
| Case studies | Medium |
| Opinions/takes | Low |
| Quick tips | Low |

## How to Get Comments

### Comment Triggers
1. **Ask questions** - End with "👇"
2. **Ask for opinions** - "Agree or disagree?"
3. **Invite stories** - "Tag someone..."
4. **Polarizing takes** - Controversial opinions
5. **Admit mistakes** - "Don't make my mistake..."

### Question Templates
- "What's YOUR biggest [PAIN POINT]?"
- "Which [X] or [Y]? Drop below."
- "Who's with me? 👇"
- "What's your take on [TOPIC]?"
- "Drop a [EMOJI] if you agree."

## How to Get Shares

### Share-Worthy Content
- **Useful guides** - Worth sending to team
- **Tools lists** - Worth sharing with peers
- **Truth bombs** - Worth sending to friends
- **Contraversial takes** - Spark debate

### Share CTAs
- "Send this to [FRIEND WHO NEEDS IT]"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- "Share with your team"
- "Forward to a friend"

## First Hour is Critical

### Why First Hour Matters
- **Algorithm tests** initial engagement
- **Fast response** = strong signal
- **First hour performance** dictates reach

### What to Do in First Hour
- [ ] **Reply to ALL comments** (within 30 min)
- [ ] **Engage with 10+ other accounts**
- [ ] **Add to stories** if relevant
- [ ] **Check DMs** for responses
- [ ] **Like relevant content** in niche

## Carousel-Specific Tips

### Why Carousels Win (2026)
- **1.4x-3x more reach** than single posts
- **Higher save rate** - people save entire carousel
- **Swipe interaction** = double engagement
- **Story potential** - can screenshot to stories

### Carousel Best Practices
1. **First slide MUST stop scroll**
2. **Each slide valuable alone**
3. **Use numbers** - "5 ways" format works
4. **End with CTA** - Save + comment
5. **5-10 slides** optimal

---

## Reels-Specific Tips

### Reels in 2026
- **Still highest reach** potential
- **20% view rate** for smaller accounts
- **Algorithm favors** original audio
- **Hook in 1-3 seconds** critical

### Reels Best Practices
1. **Hook in first 1-3 seconds**
2. **Use trending audio** when relevant
3. **Create covers** - 1080x1920 vertical
4. **Reply to comments** in next day's content
5. **Post 2-3x weekly**

```

```

## What Kills Engagement

### ❌ Avoid
1. **Low-quality images** - Blurry, pixelated
2. **No value** - Content without substance
3. **Spammy hashtags** - Irrelevant, overstuffed
4. **No CTA** - Don't ask, don't get
5. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm punishes
6. **Ignoring comments** - Death sentence
7. **Buying followers** - Fake engagement
