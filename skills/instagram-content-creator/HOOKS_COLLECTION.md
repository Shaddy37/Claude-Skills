# 🪝 Hook Collection - Instagram Hooks

## Category A: Save-Focused (Highest Saves)

These hooks create "save-worthy" expectation immediately.

1. `Save this post for later...`
2. `Save this. You'll need it when [SITUATION].`
3. `The only [TOPIC] guide you'll ever need.`
4. `Bookmark this. Trust me.`
5. `Save this before you forget.`
6. `This is your sign to [ACTION].`
7. `Don't scroll past this.`
8. `If you [GOAL], save this post.`
9. `The [NUMBER] things I wish I knew about [TOPIC].`
10. `Everything you need to know about [TOPIC].`

## Hook Selection Guide

| Goal | Best Hooks |
|------|-----------|
| Saves | Category A |
| Reach (Reels) | Category B |
| Education | Category C |
| Engagement | Category F |
| Authority | Category D + E |
| Personal Brand | Category G |

---

## How to Use

1. **Pick hook FIRST** - Before creating content
2. **Match format** - Some hooks work better for carousels vs Reels
3. **Test different hooks** - Track which get more saves
4. **Build your own** - Mix categories for unique hooks
5. **Keep hook promise** - Deliver on the hook in content

---

*Use these hooks as starting points. Best hooks are specific to your niche and audience.*
