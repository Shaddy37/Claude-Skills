## Call-to-Action Templates for Instagram

## CTA Selection Guide

| Post Type | Best CTA | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Carousel | "Save this" | High saves |
| Reels | "Follow for more" | Growth |
| Single | Question | Comments |
| Stories | Reply | Engagement |

---

## CTA Categories

### Soft CTAs (Saves/Awareness)
1. "Save this post for later 📌"
2. "Save this. You'll thank me later."
3. "Bookmark this guide."
4. "Save if you want to automate your business."

### Medium CTAs (Engagement)
1. "What's YOUR biggest time drain? 👇"
2. "Comment below - I read every one"
3. "Tag someone who needs this"
4. "Share with your network"

### Hard CTAs (Conversion)
1. "Message me with 'automate' for free consultation"
2. "Ready to automate? DM me"
3. "Book a free call: [link]"

---

## CTA by Format

### Carousel CTAs
- "Save this for later 📌"
- "Bookmark this guide"
- "Save if you found value"

### Reels CTAs
- "Follow for more automation tips 💜"
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- "Share with your team"

### Single Post CTAs
- "What's your take? 👇"
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "Drop your answer below"

### Stories CTAs
- "Reply with your thoughts"
- "Vote in poll"
- "Send me a DM"
