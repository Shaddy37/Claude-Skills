# 🎠 Carousel Templates

## What Makes a Viral Carousel

- **First slide stops the scroll**
- **Clear value promise**
- **Numbered progression**
- **Consistent design**
- **End with CTA**

## Carousel Design Rules

### Visual Consistency
- Same background color
- Same font style
- Same text position
- Numbered progression

### Text Rules
- 3-5 words per slide max
- Use icons/emoji
- High contrast colors
- Big readable text

### Color Schemes
- Dark mode: #1a1a2e background, #00d4ff accent
- Light mode: #FFFFFF background, #1a1a2e text
- Use accent colors for highlights

---

## Best Practices

1. **First slide MUST stop the scroll**
2. **Make each slide valuable alone**
3. **Use consistent design**
4. **End with clear CTA**
5. **Match caption to slides**
6. **Include save CTA**
7. **Use 5-10 slides max**

*Use these templates as starting points. Customize for your specific content and audience.*
