## 📋 How to Track

### Weekly Process

1. **Identify**: Find 5-10 competitors in your niche
2. **Follow**: Connect and follow their content
3. **Analyze**: Review their top posts weekly
4. **Document**: Record what works
5. **Apply**: Use insights in your content

---

## 🔍 What to Analyze

### For Each Competitor Post

| Element | Look For |
|---------|----------|
| **Hook** | What grabs attention? |
| **Format** | Carousel, text, video? |
| **Topic** | What's trending? |
| **CTA** | How do they convert? |
| **Engagement** | Comments, saves, shares |
| **Timing** | When do they post? |
