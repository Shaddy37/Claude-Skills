## Formatting Rules

- **Hook**: First 200 characters must grab attention
- **Length**: 1300-2000 characters
- **Lines**: 8-12 words max per line
- **Paragraphs**: 2-3 lines max
- **Emojis**: 3-5 per post
- **Questions**: End with specific questions
