## Best Practices

1. **Front-load the result** - Put the money/time saved first
2. **Be specific** - Use exact numbers, not "a lot"
3. **Tell a story** - Challenge → Solution → Result
4. **Include tools** - Show what you used (n8n, Claude, etc.)
5. **End with CTA** - Lead to conversion
