## 🎯 CTA Categories

### Soft CTAs (Awareness Stage)

**Purpose**: Start conversation, don't sell

```
1. "My DMs are open if you want to chat"
2. "Drop a comment below - I read every one"
3. "Save this post for later"
4. "Follow for more [niche] content"
5. "What's YOUR experience with this?"
6. "Tag someone who needs to see this"
7. "Share this if it helped"
```

### Medium CTAs (Engagement Stage)

**Purpose**: Capture leads, start relationship

```
1. "Comment 'automate' and I'll send my free AI Automation Checklist"
2. "Need help with [TOPIC]? Let's talk"
3. "What's your biggest time drain? Reply and I'll give my thoughts"
4. "Save this, then DM me your biggest challenge"
5. "Drop your [PAIN POINT] below - I'll help you think through it"
6. "Comment below and I'll share [VALUE] with you"
7. "Save this post and message me with 'automation' for free consultation"
```

### Hard CTAs (Conversion Stage)

**Purpose**: Close leads, generate revenue

```
1. "Ready to automate? Book a free 15-min call"
2. "Want similar results? Message me with 'automate'"
3. "Ready to scale? Let's discuss your project"
4. "Ready to cut costs? Schedule a free discovery call"
5. "DM me 'start' and let's talk automation"
6. "Book your free consultation: [LINK]"
7. "Ready to transform your business? Let's talk"
```

## ✅ CTA Best Practices

1. **Match CTA to post goal**: Educational = save, Case study = conversion
2. **Don't over-CTA**: Every post doesn't need hard sell
3. **Test and track**: Which CTAs get responses?
4. **Be specific**: "Comment 'automate'" > "Contact me"
5. **Make it easy**: Give clear action
